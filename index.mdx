## What can you build?

The Roulette API enables you to:

- **Automate deal flow** - Programmatically add companies from forms, emails, or other sources
- **Sync data** - Keep your internal systems in sync with your Roulette pipeline
- **Build integrations** - Connect Roulette with your existing tools and workflows
- **Generate reports** - Pull data for custom analytics and reporting
- **Manage sharing** - Create and manage shared links for LPs and co-investors
